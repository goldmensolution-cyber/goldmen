## Supported Networks

Goldmen Solutions allows you to buy airtime for **all major Kenyan networks**:

- Safaricom  
- Airtel Kenya  
- Telkom Kenya  
- Equitel  
- Faiba  

## Transaction Limits

| Network | Minimum (KES) | Maximum (KES) |
|---------|---------------|---------------|
| Safaricom | 10 | 10,000 |
| Airtel    | 10 | 10,000 |
| Telkom    | 10 | 5,000 |
| Equitel   | 10 | 5,000 |
| Faiba     | 10 | 3,000 |

## Notes

- Multiple purchases can be made in a day, subject to M-Pesa daily limits.  
- For bulk airtime or corporate purchases, contact our sales team.
