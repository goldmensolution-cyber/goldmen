## Overview

This document shows you how to use Goldmen’s **online airtime purchase form** to instantly send airtime.

## Steps

1. **Navigate to "Buy Online"** — Visit the Goldmen homepage and click **Buy Online**.  
2. **Fill in the Form** — Provide:
   - **Phone number** receiving airtime
   - **Network provider** (Safaricom, Airtel, Telkom, Equitel, or Faiba)
   - **Amount** to purchase  
3. **Submit** — Click the submit button.  
4. **Payment Step** — Complete payment using your preferred method (M-Pesa, card, etc.).  
5. **Confirmation & Delivery** — You’ll get a confirmation, then airtime is delivered instantly.  

## Troubleshooting

- **Form errors?** Ensure the phone number is valid and the amount is within allowed limits.  
- **No airtime received?** Contact our support with your form reference or order number.

## FAQs

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Can I send airtime to any network? | Yes—form supports all listed networks. |
| Is it secure? | Yes—payments are processed securely through M-Pesa or other gateway. |
