## Overview

This guide walks you through purchasing airtime using the **M-Pesa Paybill** method on Goldmen Solutions.

## Steps

1. **Open M-Pesa** — Launch the M-Pesa app or standard SIM toolkit menu on your phone.  
2. **Select "Lipa na M-Pesa" → "Paybill"** — Enter our Business Number: **4166283**.  
3. **Enter Account Number** — Use your phone number (or another recipient’s) as the account number.  
4. **Enter Amount** — Input the airtime amount you wish to purchase.  
5. **Confirm Payment** — Review the details and send. You’ll receive an M-Pesa confirmation SMS.  
6. **Receive Airtime** — Airtime is sent instantly. If not, contact support.

## Troubleshooting

- **Payment failed or declined?** Check your M-Pesa balance and try again. If the issue persists, contact support with your transaction reference.
- **Wrong account number entered?** Airtime cannot be reversed once processed. Double-check before confirming.

## FAQs

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Can I buy airtime for other people? | Yes—just enter the recipient’s number as the account number. |
| Are there extra charges? | No—Goldmen only charges the airtime cost, no hidden fees. |
