## Do I Need an Account?

- **No account required** — You can buy airtime directly through Paybill or our online form.  
- **Optional account** — Creating an account lets you:
  - Save frequent numbers  
  - View purchase history  
  - Access faster checkout  

## How We Protect Your Data

- All transactions are encrypted with **SSL**.  
- We never store your M-Pesa PIN or sensitive payment details.  
- Phone numbers are only used for airtime delivery.  

## Security Tips

- Always confirm you’re on the official site: **[https://goldmen.co.ke](https://goldmen.co.ke)**  
- Double-check recipient numbers before confirming.  
- Do not share your M-Pesa PIN with anyone.  
- If you suspect fraud, contact us immediately at **support@goldmen.co.ke**.  

## FAQs

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Is my payment secure? | Yes—payments are processed safely through M-Pesa and licensed providers. |
| Can someone hack my account? | Accounts are protected with secure logins; always use a strong password. |
| Do I need to log in every time? | No—you can buy airtime without logging in. Login is only for tracking and convenience. |
