## Overview

Sometimes, transactions may fail or airtime may not be delivered. This guide explains our refund and troubleshooting process.

## Common Scenarios

- **Payment deducted but no airtime delivered**  
- **Wrong number entered**  
- **Duplicate transaction**  

## What to Do

1. **Save Your M-Pesa SMS** — Keep the confirmation message safe.  
2. **Contact Support** — Email support@goldmen.co.ke or call our helpline with:
   - Your M-Pesa transaction ID  
   - The phone number you intended to top-up  
   - Time and date of purchase  
3. **Resolution** — Our team verifies your payment:
   - If valid, airtime is resent to the correct number.  
   - If not possible, we initiate a refund.  

## Policy Notes

- Refunds are only possible if airtime hasn’t been delivered.  
- Wrong recipient numbers cannot be reversed—always double-check.
