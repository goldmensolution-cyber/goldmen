## Overview

After buying airtime, you may want to confirm whether your purchase was processed successfully. This guide shows you how.

## Steps

1. **Check Your SMS** — You should receive two confirmations:
   - An **M-Pesa SMS** confirming payment.  
   - A **Goldmen SMS** confirming airtime delivery.  
2. **Check Your Airtime Balance** — Dial your provider’s USSD code (e.g., `*144#` for Safaricom).  
3. **Check Your Email or Account** — If you purchased via the online form while logged in, you’ll also get an email confirmation.  
4. **Still Unsure?** — Contact support with your **M-Pesa transaction ID** or **form order reference**.

## FAQs

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| How long does airtime delivery take? | Usually instant, but may take up to 2 minutes during peak hours. |
| What if I got the M-Pesa SMS but no airtime? | Share the transaction ID with support—we’ll verify and resend if needed. |
